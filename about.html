International and EU students

You will find a diverse mix of people from different backgrounds, faiths, nationalities and ages all sharing the close-knit City Campus.

This mixture of people in a compact space creates a vibrant community atmosphere where lifelong friendships are made between students from all over the world.

Choosing to study outside your home country is an exciting prospect, and a big decision. We want to help you make the right choice.
